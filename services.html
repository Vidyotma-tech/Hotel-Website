Services

We, at Leela Mahal, are committed to provide our guests with the best service to ensure a day well spent. Whether you are travelling for business or pleasure, the luxury hotel services offered by the five star Leela Mahal make it an ideal choice for your stay in Jaipur, India.

Add-on Services by Leela Mahal

Exprience the true treasures of life, after all it is all about the little joys. It's the little memories that will last a lifetime. These add-on luxury experiences are prepared by Leela Mahal upon prior bookings.

  • Touching Senses with Yoga

    Check out from the bustle of life and take some time to reconnect with your inner self. Surya namskara, dhyana flow yoga, hatha yoga, power yoga and more in the serene morning weather.
    Note: This experience is managed by a third party professional yoga and pilates studio.

    Price INR 2,000 per person.

    Dine Under The Stars

    A specially crafted meal for two, served by a personal butler, under the stars and beside cascading water bodies. Choose between a specially curated four course dinner and our recommended Royal Indian ‘thali’. Delicious cuisines to inspire a special occasion.
    Note: Beverages are not included in the price.

    Price INR 12,000 per person.

    Camel Cart Ride

    Climb aboard a traditional, painted camel cart and let us take you on a jaunt along the tree lined routes near our luxury resort. Pass by local people going about their work, past surrounding vegetation and back to the resort in comfort atop your private perch.
    Duration: 45 minutes.

    Price INR 5,000 for a couple.

  • Rejuvenating Spa for Couples

    Experience a full body massage with richly aromatic attar oils using traditional kasa bowls. The experience concludes with our signature dhara, a continuous, warm, aromatic flow onto the forehead, which calms the third eye and promotes a profound sense of relaxation.
    Duration: 3 hours.

    Price INR 30,000 for a couple.

    Spice Talk in Sandhya Market

    A specially curated tour of Sandhya Market on the back of a customised rickshaw, accompanied by a guide. Discover the secrets of herbs and spices used in Indian cuisine. Enjoy a connoisseur's guide to our native spices and learn about their medicinal properties.
    Timings: 5.00pm to 6.00pm, every Friday.

    Price INR 4,000 per person.

    Kids DayCare

    Your kids can be in our care while you spend some quality time with your better half. The daycare center has a separate children's pool and an ultra-thin, roofed shady outdoor pool coupled with a sand box ideal for small children.
    Age: From 4 to 10 years old

    Price INR 1,000 per kid.

  • Indian Wine Tasting

    A tour of the Indian subcontinent’s vineyards through its wines. Learn about our indigenous grapes, how to discern bouquets and choose wines to suit different cuisines. If you are a budding sommelier, our in-house wine specialist will help cultivate the expert in you.
    Duration: 1 hour.

    Price INR 2,500 per person.

    Astrology

    The ancient science of astrology uses precise mathematical tools to map the alignment of the planets at the time and place of your birth; Our professional astrologer helps in revealing the hidden secrets of your personality and what the future has in store.
    Duration: 30 mins

    Price INR 1,500 per person.

    Henna Painting

    Sit back, relax and prepare to be amazed as a skilled mehendi artist deftly embellishes your hands or feet with intricate patterns using natural inks, henna, a non allergenic natural dye. If your skin is particularly sensitive, please consult our concierge before booking.
    Duration: 30 minutes for one hand.

    Price INR 1,000 per hand.

Facilities

We are geared towards the fulfilment of the needs of any discerning guest and below you can find an overview of the most commonly-used services and facilities offered by our boutique hotel.



Afternon Garden Parties

The Leela Mahal has verdant gardens and our in-house horticulturalist would be happy to show you around them. From our centenarian raintree to the frangipani and bougainvillea. Followed by tea on the garden to experience a fascinating journey through the world of Indian teas. Unravel the herb and spice mixtures used in different tea preparations and learn how to brew traditional Indian masala chai, Kashmiri kahwa or South Indian filter coffee.

Retail Therapy

Spend some time at our luxury retail arcade, situated on the lobby and lower lobby levels of the hotel. The arcade offers exclusive shopping experiences and personalised service. Hand spun scarves, shawls and silk carpets from Kashmir . Italian luxury menswear. Customised flower arrangements crafted from fresh Indian and imported blooms. Hair styling with toxin free, eco friendly products for ladies and gentlemen. Fine Indian art and antiquities. Traditional and contemporary jewellery. Luxury teas. Pick up the perfect souvenir of your stay in India, or treat someone special to a unique gift.

Breakfast Buffet

With its pleated silk walls, plush ecru chairs, red roses, and of course crystal chandeliers and candelabras, the Baccarat Hotel’s Grand Salon will get you in the mood for a decadent breakfast. Spread across the vast 18,000-square-foot dining room were multiple live kitchens devoted to specialty items and regional cuisines. Orange flames tended by chefs clad in white coats, sizzling batter on hot griddles, clattering dishes, and fragrant air of spice and smoke, all lending a dramatic spectacle. It is breakfast theater at its finest.

Comfortable Stay

Choose from 220 brightly lit, tastefully decorated and comfortably furnished rooms and suites with spacious walk in closets, Italian marble bathrooms, teak floors and oak wood desks. Breathe easy with state of the art air purification systems that assure air quality on par with global best standards. In-depth cleanliness and disinfection protocol is designed to ensure your safety and peace of mind from check-in to check-out.

Cocktail Bar

A drink at the Leela Mahal Bar is much more than just a drink. This cocktail bar houses award-winning wines, top-shelf spirits and the finest of accompaniments. Be it for business or pleasure, sit down, unwind and have a memorable time at this elegant bar. Choose from an exhaustive and award-winning range of 240 wine and champagne labels, 100 brands of single malts and premium whiskies, and light up the finest cigars as you host your most innovative cocktail and business parties and social rendezvous.
